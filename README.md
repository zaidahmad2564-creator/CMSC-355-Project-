------------------ Project Information ------------------

The purpose of this project is to create an easy-to-use medical application that simplifies healthcare management for both patients and providers. The app will allow patients to conveniently schedule, manage, and cancel appointments, as well as organize their prescriptions. For healthcare providers, it will support the creation and management of patient records and prescriptions, offering an all-in-one solution for efficient healthcare administration.

---- Functional Requirements ----

FR01: Appointment Management
The application will enable patients to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments with ease. Healthcare providers will also be able to manage their availability and control appointment slots.

FR02: Prescription Management
Healthcare providers will have the ability to create, update, and remove prescriptions based on patient needs. Patients will be able to request prescription refills when necessary.

FR03: Medical History Management
Providers will be able to create, update, and manage patient medical records. Patients and authorized guardians will have access to view these medical histories.

---- Non-Functional Requirements ----

NFR01: Timely Appointment Updates
Appointment changes and cancellations must be processed on the same day they are requested to ensure timely updates.

NFR02: Data Security and Privacy
Patient medical records and personal information will be accessible only to authorized individuals, including the patient and their healthcare provider, to maintain security and confidentiality.
